Dion Fortune's Book of the Dead  March 2017

Rereading Dion Fortune's Book of the Dead I found a few nuggets of useful information. I'm rereading it because the language of the 1930's is difficult to read as it is laced with religious and other time period references. For example, there are lots of references to God, Christ, angels as if these are facts. Also, not many of us know what a coal-scuttle is anymore. But reading around all of that, there is some interesting information such as her description of the process of dying.
 ★★☆☆☆
