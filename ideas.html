Book and Video Ideas

PI Jobe Alpha Klean  Thursday March 31, 2016

While trying out a new baptist church, of all places, I had an idea for a detective like story. The story is about the life of a private investigator (PI) named Jobe Klean. That's pronounced Jobe like Job the book in the Old Testament. He is not licensed and therefore does not work as an official PI, rather he is stubborn and curious about people. His cases involve work that is beyond what a typical PI would do, but he likes to think he always gets his man. He is unarmed, because he does not believe weapons are needed. He is not aggressive yet he is relentless about solving a case. He worked for more than twenty years at the Federal Bureau of Investigation as a computer scientist. So he does have a somewhat related police background.

Jobe is absolutely nothing like Sherlock Holmes. He does not possess a great intellect and is not well read. Nor does he have a doctor or any other partner. He is a loner. But he is stubborn, sometimes to a fault, and will always see a case through until the end.

This story may be better presented as a series of YouTube videos instead of as a book with tons of dialogue. I am not sure I can write that much interesting dialogue.

How to do this as series of videos?
  • Add in his history/background using flash backs or whatever
  • Do I play all of the characters? How? Masks or clothing? ... sounds dumb
  • Never show Jobe, but do everything through his eyes
  • Film everything at or near home
  • How long should each episode be? 10 minutes or less
  • Should it have an ASMR quality? i.e. it's somewhat relaxing to watch
  • Speak quietly, softly, rhythmically
  • Keep ASMR in mind when handling objects, except for that annoying tapping
  • Keep the video quality amateurish, well, not as bad as the Blair Witch Project
  • Mix together both live video and screenshots of case notes, news, etc.
  • Have a paper case file? technically for testifying in court, but actually for touching with ASMR in mind
