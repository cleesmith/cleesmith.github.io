RWS Tarot

The following is a tarot deck of cards from about 1909. This deck was created by Arthur Edward Waite and Pamela Colman Smith (Pixie). It was printed by William Rider & Son in Britain, hence the three-letter acronym of RWS (Rider Waite Smith).
You may click/tap the "pull" button to randomly select up to seven cards one card at a time. No spreads are offered nor interpretations of the cards given ... all of that is up to you, but there are a lot of online resources regarding tarot.
