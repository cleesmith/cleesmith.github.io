Changing the world

you know, impossible ideas

Solutions  Wednesday March 30, 2016

Any solutions must be free and available to everyone on the planet.

Energy

Replace or make free oil/electric/gas energy and remove existing pollution.

Travel

Create a Star Trek like transporter to offer free travel.

Communication

Create a universal translator to remove culture/language barriers, i.e. everyone understands everyone else.

Poverty

Provide clean fresh water and healthy food ... all free.

Freedom

Remove money/trade ... everything is free.

Agression

No aggression, i.e. weapons no longer could harm and no one can have harmful intentions.

How would all of these changes create a world that is worst than it is now?

How could someone with evil intentions take advantage of this new world? Should something like an implant kill them the moment their intentions are known?

Is the implant a form of terrorism or source of fear? Is it too much? But without an implant how would we control those with bad intentions? The implant should be like a virus, or DNA change, so there's no need to to police everyone. It's a world wide infection, so to speak, which immediately affects the thoughts and behaviors of everyone. It's past on to children at birth. It fixes our weaker human qualities by prohibiting us from doing those things. Imagine a world without the fear of other humans, no hunger, and no suffering. The conscious cop virus (CCV) would alleviate this need to repeat the mistakes and suffering of the past. There's no cure for the virus as it is the cure. Do no harm (DNH) virus ... a better name?
