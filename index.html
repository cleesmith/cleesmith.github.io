website rant

Why are there so many websites where the meat of the page is totally surrounded by ad's, tiny videos, follow us, and other annoying garbage? I guess the "word" is not as powerful as we've been told. We actually came to their page to read the article, and not to be bombarded by ad's, pop-up's, or whatever. It gets worse as you scroll down while reading -- pop up's occur or videos start playing or the page refreshes parts of itself and becomes unresponsive.

It is so damn annoying!
My usual reaction is to immediately close the page. There should be a web browser setting that can only load the readable text on a page and nothing else. You know, like the old days of the internet. Ok, I found Textise which is slow but makes annoying websites more readable.
