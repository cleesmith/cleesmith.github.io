Hardware Experience

mainframe:
every IBM 360, 370, etc. -- basically everything IBM has made starting with the 360 and beyond

can you imagine:
- disk drives and tape silos that are across the room from the cpu
    - now that's speed and storage -- makes PC's seem mundane and useless
- all in a special built data center with:
    - cables the size of your arm hidden under the floor
    - a temperature that's so cold you could hang meat on hooks
    - halon gas dispensers to stop fires (WTF? who needs an ozone layer)
Now that is serious computing with unlimited power, resources, and a budget for such. Entering that room, well, it felt a bit like meeting the source in the Matrix.
