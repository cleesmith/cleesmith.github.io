PI Job Clean  Thursday March 31, 2016

While trying out a new baptist church, of all places, I had an idea for a detective like story. The story is about the life of a private investigator (PI) named Job Clean. That's pronounced Job as in the book in the Old Testament, but most people call him Jo. He is not licensed and therefore does not work as an official PI, rather he is more like a spy. His cases involve work that is beyond what the law allows. But he is known for getting results. He is unarmed, because he does not believe weapons are needed. He is not aggressive yet he is relentless about solving a case. He worked for more than twenty years at the Federal Bureau of Investigation as a computer scientist. So he does have a somewhat related police background.
