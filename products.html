Why offer and sell an ebook-in-progress?

In short, this “idea” to offer an ebook-in-progress is an incentive, for me, to finish writing the stories of Taropian Songs. It’s a bit like a promise made to someone, which I think will encourage me to focus more on writing it. Writing isn’t easy, probably not for anyone but definitely not for me, so I need a carrot. Even if only a single soul pays $1.99, I think that will push me along through the images and stories. Currently, I am writing The Empress story, and so, I have a very long ways to go.

Full Disclosure or Too Long; Didn’t Read (TL;DR)

While this may seem a bit Kickstarter-like, it’s not, because it’s only $1.99, a one-time price, forever. Plus you get something now! Well, unless the interwebs are down. Well, it's not a done something, but a something in progress. And you can always email me your opinion like: "What's the hold up? I've got a dollar and 99 cents on the line here!" Ok, I am not really picking on Kickstarter, as I do like the original mission idea of crowdfunding; to "help bring creative projects to life". However, it seems that over time, with increased costs, project delays, and so on; the original lofty goals may have gone astray. IMHO. Hence this idea of mine. As this writing progresses, it is possible that I may create a few non-public YouTube videos, or offer some other bonuses that come to mind.

Also, this idea is an alternative to Kindle Vella, which currently does NOT:
   - offer images along with words, so no graphic novels
   - allow readers outside USA, so Vella is not worldwide
Yes, there are other paywall options on the internet like Wattpad or Patreon, but all of those seemed either expensive or forced a buyer to choose a subscription service. I am not a fan of subscription service, hence this idea.

This offer gets you worldwide 24/7 access (ignoring interweb outages) to a webpage with full resolution images, lots of words placed carefully beside each other, with no mispellings, and with no wonky English grammar ... all thanks to ProWritingAid and Grammarly.

As for the storytelling aspect and the enjoyability of the reading experience, I will leave that to you to decide. Yes, through PayPal a full refund is available within 180 days of payment, which is rather generous. Just email me if you want a refund anytime within that timeframe. No questions asked, as they say.

If you wish, please share your thoughts, reviews, or whatevers via an email directly with the author, me. Any communication will be in private and confidential. While all such sharing is welcomed, it may not influence nor improve my writing. 😏

No separate app is required as this “idea” uses a simple webpage viewed in your existing web browser. However, you may prefer a PDF, just send me an email asking for one. The PDF will also have the full resolution images, in color, which are the exact images used to print the tarot deck.
Note: the PDF file will be encryted and password protected, as is this online version.

Any new writing appears as episodes on Kindle Vella first, only USA, then each will appear here for reading. Why? Because Kindle Vella is currently, as of October 2022, paying a monthly bonus in real money; just for writing and publishing with them, and without any quality review process, nor does anyone actually have to read it; the bonus money seems to be just for publishing alone … welcome to “Bizarro World”.

When completed, a story will be paired alongside all 79 images i.e. the fronts of all cards plus the back. Eventually, this writing will become a regular Amazon book; Kindle and paperback. When that day arrives, I will offer a discount for “early readers” who paid $1.99, as in a free Kindle ebook.

Here's hoping to see you somewhere along this long path ahead, enjoy! ☮
